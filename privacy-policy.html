Privacy Policy - Desktop Uploader

Last updated: 19 June 2025

1. Who we are

Desktop Uploader (“the App”) is a desktop tool that helps creators send finished MP4 files to their TikTok Drafts folder for later publishing.

2. Data we collect

  • OAuth tokens for TikTok – stored only on your device, never on our servers.
  • Video files and metadata you explicitly choose to upload.

3. How we use data

Tokens authorise uploads; video files are transmitted only to official platform APIs. We do not sell or share your data.

4. Third-party services

TikTok Content Posting API

5. Security

All API calls use HTTPS. Tokens are encrypted via your OS keychain (Keychain on macOS, DPAPI on Windows).

6. Your rights

You may revoke platform access at any time in the App or in platform security settings. Delete local data by removing the App’s config folder.

7. Changes

We will update this page for any future changes. Continuing to use the App after an update constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
