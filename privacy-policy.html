Privacy Policy – Desktop Uploader

Last updated: 19 June 2025

1. Who we are

The Desktop Uploader is a tool that helps creators upload videos directly to their TikTok profiles through TikTok's official API.

2. Data we collect

  • OAuth tokens for TikTok – stored only on your device, never on our servers.
  • Video files and metadata that you explicitly choose to upload.

3. How we use data

We use OAuth tokens solely to authorize uploads to TikTok. All videos and metadata are sent directly to TikTok via their official Content Posting API. We do not sell, share, or store your data on any external servers.

4. Third-party services

This app integrates exclusively with the TikTok Content Posting API for direct content publishing.

5. Security

All API requests use secure HTTPS connections. OAuth tokens are encrypted using your operating system’s secure keychain (e.g., Keychain on macOS or DPAPI on Windows).

6. Your rights

You may revoke TikTok access tokens at any time via the app or your TikTok security settings. You may also delete all local data by removing the app’s configuration files.

7. Changes

We may update this policy at any time. Continued use of the app after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
